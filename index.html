Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


We offer a variety of loan options, including Personal Loans, Educational Loans, Home Loans, Mortgage Loans, and more. Each loan is tailored to meet specific financial needs.

You can apply online by filling out our loan application form on the website. Alternatively, you can visit our office or contact our support team for assistance.

The required documents vary by loan type but generally include:

Personal Loan: Identity proof, address proof, income proof, and bank statements. Educational Loan: Admission letter, fee structure, identity proof, and co-applicant details. Home Loan: Property documents, income proof, identity proof, and address proof. Mortgage Loan: Property papers, financial statements, and KYC documents.

Loan approval timelines vary based on the type of loan and document verification. Typically, personal and educational loans are processed within 24–72 hours, while home and mortgage loans may take 7–15 working days.

The loan amount depends on your eligibility, income, and credit profile. We offer loan amounts ranging from ₹50,000 to several crores, depending on the type of loan.

Interest rates vary based on the loan type, borrower’s credit score, and other factors. Please contact our team or check our website for the latest interest rates.

For certain loans like educational or high-value mortgage loans, a guarantor or co-applicant may be required. Personal loans typically do not require a guarantor.

Yes, we allow loan prepayment and foreclosure. However, some loans may have prepayment charges. Please check the loan terms for details.

You can track your loan application status through our website by logging into your account or by contacting our customer support team.

Missing an EMI payment can result in late fees, penalties, and a negative impact on your credit score. We recommend setting up automatic payments to avoid delays.
